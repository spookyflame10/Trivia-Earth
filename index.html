About the program: 'Trivia Earth' is an educational game that intends to save the world one tree at a time, while helping people improve their mental math skills as well as increasing their range of knowledge. For every 10 questions consecutively answered correctly, one tree is planted somewhere in the world. Game created by Joy Yang, Dylan Lai, Juan Vazquez, and Simon Lee.
